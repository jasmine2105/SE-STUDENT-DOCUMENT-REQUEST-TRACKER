Student Document Request Tracker - USJR

Last Updated: September 2024

1. User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials and for all activities that occur under their account.

2. Data Privacy

All personal information collected through this system is used solely for processing document requests and is handled in accordance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act No. 10173).

3. Request Processing

Document requests are processed within 3-5 business days. Rush processing may be available for an additional fee.

4. Prohibited Activities

Users are prohibited from submitting false information, impersonating others, or using the system for any fraudulent purposes.

5. Email Notifications

Users will receive email notifications regarding their request status updates to the email address provided during registration.

6. System Availability

We strive to maintain 24/7 system availability but cannot guarantee uninterrupted access. Scheduled maintenance will be announced in advance.

7. Contact Information

For support or inquiries, please contact the Registrar's Office or visit registrar@usjr.edu.ph
