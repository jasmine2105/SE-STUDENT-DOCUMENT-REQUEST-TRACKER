Common Document Types

Transcript of Records (TOR)

Official academic transcript showing all completed courses and grades.

Certificate of Enrollment

Proof of current enrollment status for scholarships, internships, and other purposes.

Good Moral Certificate

Character reference and conduct certificate for employment and further studies.

Certificate of Graduation

Official certificate confirming completion of degree requirements.

Recommendation Letter

Academic or character recommendation letters from faculty members.

Clearance

Academic and administrative clearance for graduation and other purposes.
